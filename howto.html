For HELP point here!

This small webapp was designed to provide a reference point for prices and 4C parameters of diamonds. The data was taken from the ggplot2 package.

In order to compare the diamond of your selection with multiple reference diamonds in the database:

  • Select weight
  • Choose your price range
  • Toggle the clarity and cut that you find acceptable
  • Compare the price!

Should you decide to hover over a selected point, it will display the precise information abou the selected diamond. Also, you can change axis and fill color in the panel under the plot.
